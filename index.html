Meet Morgan

Morgan Blair (he/him/his) is a senior SMAD major at James Madison University (JMU) with a concentration in journalism. Morgan is the print managing editor for JMU's student run newspaper, The Breeze, where he manages personell issues, edits and writes articles, and helps to layout both the physical and the digital paper each week.
